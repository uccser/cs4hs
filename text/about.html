About

There are two independent CS4HS workshops running throughout New Zealand in 2017. Our workshops in Christchurch are focused on the Computer Science and Programming Achievement standards for teachers who are at beginner/intermediate level, so the discussions will be more focussed on getting started.

There is also a CS4HS event at the Victoria University of Wellington, on 13th to 15th of December. See their CS4HS website for more details. The events are independent and are covering different areas, so it's fine to apply to go to more than one (if you have the time!).
