Heads up!

This year CS4HS is quite different to previous years. The PD landscape for DT in NZ has changed a lot, with a rich range of offerings now available. To help us avoid spreading ourselves too thin, CS4HS at Canterbury this year will be aimed at teachers taking their "first steps" in teaching Computer Science and Programming; hence priority will be given to teachers attending for the first time.

For more details read the full announcement.

Welcome to the website for the Christchurch Computer Science for High Schools event, held at the University of Canterbury in 2017. The event is a collection of workshops focused on preparing New Zealand educators for teaching the computer science and programming achievement standards for NCEA. We have received funding for this event through Google's CS4HS Grant Program, and through other generous sponsors, and are running the workshops over three days from the 6th to 8th of December 2017, with optional programming workshops occurring on the 4th and 5th of December.

Thanks to generous funding, we are able to provide accommodation and food at no charge to New Zealand educators attending the main workshops. We are also able to provide travel subsidies to those travelling large distances to attend the event.

The workshops aims to cover:

  • Introduction to CS principles
  • Computer Science NCEA Standards
  • Programming NCEA Standards
  • Student stories and industry visits
  • Networking opportunities with other NZ educators

This website will tell you everything you need to know about the workshops including a full schedule, information about transport and locations for the event, and useful links to materials you may find useful throughout the workshops.

If it's your first time attending, the main workshops starts at 9am on the Wednesday with a special introduction session. If you are a returning attendee, the start time is 10:30am, however you are welcome to arrive earlier to register. The workshops also finishes officially with lunch on Friday, however an optional unconference event will be running for those with later flights.

If you are looking for information about the Wellington CS4HS event, looking to contact us, or wanting to read the FAQ, you can find all this information on the about page.
